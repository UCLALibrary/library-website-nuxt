Rich Text - Reserve a Room

CLICC Group Study Rooms

CLICC offers group study rooms in Powell Library and the Charles E. Young Research Library (YRL). UCLA students reserve a study room online and use the study room until their time is up. Group study rooms are self-service—simply walk to your study room at your appointed time with your e-mail confirmation and leave when your reservation is over. Group study rooms offer a full room with seating, power, and a screen for collaboration for up to eight people.

Powell Group Study Rooms: 6 study rooms (A–F) in the first floor reading room
YRL Group Study Rooms: 15 study rooms (G01–G15) in the first floor Research Commons
